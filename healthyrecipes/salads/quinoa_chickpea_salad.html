Lemony Quinoa Chickpea Salad

This Quinoa Chickpea Salad recipe is bursting with Mediterranean flavors – fresh light and lemony, packed full of nutrients; you’ll want to serve this up all summer long! Healthy, vegan and great for meal prep.


Ingredients ⇩

Preserved Lemon Dressing

  • 1/4 cup preserved lemons, finely chopped (or see notes)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon sumac (optional)

Chickpea Quinoa Salad

  • 3/4 cup quinoa (dry, uncooked)
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 15-ounce can chickpeas, rinsed and drained (1 1/2 cups cooked chickpeas)
  • 1/2 cup red onion, finely chopped (see notes)
  • 1 English cucumber, diced (2 cups)
  • 1 bell pepper (yellow is nice), diced
  • 1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes, halved (or chopped radish)
  • 1/4 cup kalamata olives, chopped or halved
  • 1/2 cup flat-leaf parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup dill, chopped (or sub mint, or use both!)
  • Optional additions: avocado, feta, radishes

Instructions ⇩

  1. Place quinoa, water, and a pinch of salt in a medium pot. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer on low for 12-15 minutes until all the water is gone. Uncover, fluff, and cool slightly.
  2. Make the dressing by mixing all the ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Prep all the veggies. If sensitive to onions, dice them and soak in cold salted water while you chop the rest.
  4. Place the chickpeas, onions, cucumber, bell pepper, tomatoes, olives, dill, and parsley in a large bowl. Add the quinoa and dressing and toss well.
  5. Taste and adjust salt and lemon to your liking. If making ahead, taste again before serving as the quinoa and chickpeas may soak up the salt and lemon.
