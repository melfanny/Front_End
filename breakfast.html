Greek Salad

This classic Greek salad is made with juicy tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, feta cheese, and Kalamata olives. It is seasoned with salt, pepper, olive oil, and oregano, providing a refreshing taste perfect for breakfast or a light meal.


Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 200-250 kcal
  • Total Fat: 18 grams
  • Cholesterol: 25 mg (from feta cheese)
  • Sodium: 600-800 mg (mainly from feta cheese and olives)
  • Total Carbohydrates: 10-12 grams
  • Fiber: 3-4 grams
  • Sugar: 5-6 grams (from vegetables)
  • Protein: 4-6 grams (mostly from feta cheese)

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Green bell pepper
  • Red onion
  • Cucumbers
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta Cheese

Step by Step:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash the tomatoes, green bell pepper, cucumber, and red onion thoroughly. Cut everything to the desired size (usually diced or thinly sliced).
  2. Mix the vegetables: Add the chopped tomatoes, green bell pepper, cucumber, red onion, and olives into a large bowl.
  3. Prepare the dressing: In a small bowl, mix olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined. You can also use store-bought salad dressing from the supermarket.
  4. Combine all ingredients: Pour the dressing over the salad, and gently toss everything together until all the ingredients are evenly coated.
  5. Add feta cheese: Place the crumbled or cubed feta cheese on top of the salad, without mixing it in, to keep it from breaking apart too much.
  6. Serve: The Greek salad is ready to be served as an appetizer or a side dish. Enjoy!
