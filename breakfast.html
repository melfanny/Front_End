Greek Salad

This classic Greek salad is made with juicy tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, feta cheese, and Kalamata olives. It is seasoned with salt, pepper, olive oil, and oregano, providing a refreshing taste perfect for breakfast or a light meal.


Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 200-250 kcal
  • Total Fat: 18 grams
  • Cholesterol: 25 mg (from feta cheese)
  • Sodium: 600-800 mg (mainly from feta cheese and olives)
  • Total Carbohydrates: 10-12 grams
  • Fiber: 3-4 grams
  • Sugar: 5-6 grams (from vegetables)
  • Protein: 4-6 grams (mostly from feta cheese)

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Green bell pepper
  • Red onion
  • Cucumbers
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta Cheese

Step by Step:

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash the tomatoes, green bell pepper, cucumber, and red onion thoroughly. Cut everything to the desired size (usually diced or thinly sliced).
  2. Mix the vegetables: Add the chopped tomatoes, green bell pepper, cucumber, red onion, and olives into a large bowl.
  3. Prepare the dressing: In a small bowl, mix olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir until well combined. You can also use store-bought salad dressing from the supermarket.
  4. Combine all ingredients: Pour the dressing over the salad, and gently toss everything together until all the ingredients are evenly coated.
  5. Add feta cheese: Place the crumbled or cubed feta cheese on top of the salad, without mixing it in, to keep it from breaking apart too much.
  6. Serve: The Greek salad is ready to be served as an appetizer or a side dish. Enjoy!

Diet Sandwich

This sandwich is rich in protein, fiber, and healthy fats, making it a great option for a balanced and nutritious diet.


Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 300-350 kcal
  • Total Fat: 12-15 grams (mostly from avocado and Greek yogurt)
  • Protein: 20-25 grams (from turkey/chicken and Greek yogurt)
  • Carbohydrates: 30-35 grams
  • Sodium: 500-700 mg (depends on the meat and mustard used)
  • Fiber: 5-7 grams (from whole grain bread and vegetables)
  • Sugar: 4-5 grams (from vegetables)
  • Cholesterol: 40-50 mg

Ingredients:

  • Whole grain bread (2 slices)
  • Turkey breast or grilled chicken (sliced, 2-3 oz)
  • Avocado (1/4 of a whole avocado, sliced)
  • Spinach or mixed greens (1/2 cup)
  • Tomato (1 small, sliced)
  • Cucumber (sliced thinly)
  • Low-fat Greek yogurt (1 tablespoon, as a spread)
  • Mustard (optional, 1 teaspoon)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Step by Step:

  1. Prepare the ingredients:
    • Toast the whole grain bread slices lightly if desired.
    • Slice the avocado, tomato, and cucumber.
    • Have your turkey or grilled chicken ready.
  2. Assemble the sandwich:
    • Spread the low-fat Greek yogurt on one slice of the bread. You can also spread mustard if you want more flavor.
    • Layer the turkey breast or grilled chicken on the bread.
    • Add the avocado slices, followed by the tomato slices, cucumber, and spinach or mixed greens.
    • Season with a little salt and pepper for taste.
    • Place the second slice of bread on top.
    • Optionally, slice the sandwich in half for easier handling or wrap it up for a healthy meal on the go!
